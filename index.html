MekanikOh!

Full Stack Web App

A full stack app created using MERN. Main objective for this project is to develop a site capable of giving users initial diagnosis of vehicle concerns and narrow down lists of recommended and “near your area” mechanics for services needed by clients. With capabilities such as but not limited to Online chat with mechanics for quotes, queries about certain parts / services and the likes.
